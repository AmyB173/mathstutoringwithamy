Framework and Values

Enjoy. Engage. Excel. — Our Tutoring Framework

At Maths Tutoring with Amy, we believe learning works best when students enjoy the process, actively engage with the material, and build the skills to excel. This approach guides everything we do:

Enjoy

We create a warm, supportive space where students feel comfortable and motivated. When maths feels approachable, students start to enjoy learning — even those who never thought they could.

"Amy has even helped ignite a love for maths — something our daughter never had before!"

— Sian, parent

Engage

Students learn best when they're involved and interested. Our lessons encourage questions, discussion, and problem-solving that deepen understanding and keep motivation high. We use an interactive whiteboard so that students are always actively involved, no passive learning here.

"Amy is great at getting my son engaged in the lessons at a level he understands."

— Emma, parent

Excel

We know what we're doing and teach in a way that helps students get to their goals efficiently. With steady progress and confidence building, students achieve real, lasting results.

"Amy has developed a real friendship with our daughter ….. she has progressed quicker than we thought was possible."

— N, parent

Values

People first

We treat people like people, not customers or clients. People come to us because they want more than results, they want support that's real. We get to know each student as a person, respect families, and make sure learning feels collaborative and human, never just a transaction.

Bespoke lessons

We plan every lesson specifically for each student, meeting them exactly where they are. There are no standard lessons or generic PowerPoints. Every session is unique and based on the student or students in front of us. Whether in one-to-one or small group sessions, we focus on building progress in a manageable way so students can steadily grow their confidence and understanding.

Excellence, always

We hold ourselves to the highest standards. Only tutors are all trained teachers and experienced tutors who work with our students, bringing professionalism, deep knowledge, and proven expertise. We know what we're doing, and we're committed to delivering the best possible support every time.

Honesty

We don't make false promises. If something's going to take time, we'll say so. If a student needs to hear the truth to move forward, we'll tell them kindly, but clearly. Families trust us because we're upfront about progress and prospects, every step of the way.

Progress Over Perfection

Successful people learn to love their mistakes because mistakes mean progress. We believe mistakes are the best part of learning. That's why we praise attitude, effort, and the way students approach their work, not just the final answer. Building confidence and understanding takes time, and steady progress matters more than perfection.
