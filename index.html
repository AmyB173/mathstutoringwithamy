I help girls in Year 10 and Year 11 get the top grades in the Edexcel Maths GCSE/IGCSE without doubting themselves or their abilities. After working with me, they will see mistakes as strengths, be able to quiet their self-doubt and be capable of tackling the toughest questions with a clear mind.

Is your daughter struggling to get the top grades even though you know she can do it?

Do you want your daughter to get the top grades?

I know that girls face unique challenges when it comes to Maths - and I don't mean that they are less capable. Absolutely not! Sadly, girls often doubt themselves and their abilities and this really holds them back.

I totally get it. Maths is an anxiety inducing subject and can sometimes feel daunting. But that doesn't mean that girls can't or shouldn't succeed at it. I’m passionate about transforming girls’ mindset AND results in Maths, which is why I created my programme GCSE Maths for Girls.

If your daughter doesn't get a grade 8 or 9 in her Maths GCSE, I will continue working with her for free until she does.
