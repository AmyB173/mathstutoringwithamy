Meet me

I’m Amy – an expert Maths teacher with a passion helping A Level students turn around a poor start to A Level Maths.

Has A Level Maths become a bit of a nightmare? Has the jump from GCSE to A Level surprised you? If so, please know that all is not lost. With a bit of guidance and hard work, the confidence and success can return, so don’t give up.

I have a “back-to-basics” approach where we build the solid foundation from the ground up to achieve SECURE knowledge and confidence. This is the only way to get better at the more difficult exam-style questions.
