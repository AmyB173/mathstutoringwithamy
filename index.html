My tutoring framework - Enjoy. Engage. Excel.

I've seen it time and again - students stop trying in maths not because they can't do it, but because they've convinced themselves they can't (or don't want to). That's why I build everything around helping them feel confident and engaged in their own learning.

Let's chat about how this approach can help your child

Enjoy

Lessons are calm and friendly. Making lessons fun is not making everything into a game but is about students feeling like the person teaching them is invested in them, is interested in them and talks to them like a real person. It's important students feel safe asking questions and don't worry about getting things wrong. That's when the learning really starts.

Engage

Lessons are interactive and structured, so students stay involved and are not passive learners. You will see us talking through ideas, solving problems together, and really understanding what they're doing. For students to succeed, they need to feel ownership over their progress.

Excel

Once students are happy, enjoying and engaging with the lessons, that's where the real progress can start. With my 11 years of experience, I have developed a clear and structured style of teaching, using a variety of personal and evidence-based techniques that encourages long-term retention.
