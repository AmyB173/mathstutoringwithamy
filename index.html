My tutoring framework – Enjoy. Engage. Excel.

Most students don't struggle with maths because they can't do it — they struggle because they believe they can't. This framework is all about building confidence, helping students feel involved, and setting them up to thrive.

Let's chat about how this approach can help your child

😊 Enjoy

  • Lessons are calm, warm and built on trust
  • Rapport is built by sharing interests and showing students they're seen and heard
  • We talk openly about worries so students see that struggling is normal and doesn't mean they're "bad" at maths
  • We get excited about the fun there is to be had in learning maths

🎯 Engage

  • An interactive whiteboard where students write their answers live, solve problems with me, and have their own board for homework and extra tasks
  • Lessons are active — students are always solving, thinking, or explaining
  • Thoughtful questions to make sure everyone is involved in a way that suits their confidence level

🚀 Excel

  • Low-stakes mini-assessments used to track progress and make learning stick
  • Every lesson is uniquely planned to close gaps to dramatically improve progress
  • Plus students get personalised video or voice note feedback on every homework
