Welcome to Maths Tutoring with Amy

Whether you're a parent with a child navigating their first steps into A Level Maths or an adult looking to build confidence in the subject, I'm here to support and guide you. My teaching style is all about keeping things simple and going back to basics, ensuring a strong foundation and secure understanding.

Meet Me

After a decade of being a Maths teacher, and helping hundreds of students through their Maths challenges in the classroom, I decided to turn my hand to tutoring. I decided to do this to help students using the tools I know work best not only for their grades but for them as a person too. To me, it's just as important that students feel positivity around the subject as it is to maximise the score they get on a test.

Who Do I Help?

For A Level Students

Is your child feeling overwhelmed by the jump from GCSE to A Level Maths? Have they had a tricky start and need help turning things around? Don't worry, you're not alone, and all is not lost! With my approach of taking things back-to-basics and ensuring a secure foundation in all of the Year 1 A Level content, they CAN regain their confidence and success in the subject.

Helping Adults Regain Confidence

Maths can be daunting, especially if you haven't tackled it in a while. If you're an adult feeling underconfident about your maths skills, I'm here to help you. My approach is to simplify maths concepts and provide you with the tools to grasp them easily. Maths can be daunting, especially when it's explained in an unnecessarily complicated way and I'm so confident that with better help and instruction, anyone can get better at Maths. We'll work together to increase your confidence and make learning maths an enjoyable experience.

One-on-One Tutoring for KS3, GCSE, and A Level

Every learner is unique, and so is my tutoring approach. I offer personalised one-on-one sessions for students at KS3, GCSE, and A Level, tailoring each lesson to meet your specific needs and learning pace. With patience, encouragement, and a "back-to-basics" approach, I have seen students in all circumstances make fantastic progress and improve their confidence.
