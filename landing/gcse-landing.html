About This Class

This focused group class is specifically designed for students who are aiming for the top grades in their GCSE Maths examinations (grades 7-9). It's perfect for those who want to strengthen their understanding of advanced GCSE topics and develop problem-solving skills essential for achieving excellence.

Who this group is for:

  • Year 10-11 students studying GCSE or IGCSE Mathematics
  • Students currently working at grade 5-6 who want to reach grades 7-9
  • Students already at grade 7 who want to secure grades 8-9
  • Those who benefit from structured learning in a collaborative environment

Key outcomes:

  • Master complex topics with clarity and confidence
  • Develop advanced problem-solving techniques
  • Learn exam strategies specific to higher-tier questions
  • Build a supportive peer network to enhance learning

Frequently Asked Questions

Classes follow a carefully designed curriculum to cover all essential topics for achieving grades 7-9. Each 60-minute session includes concept explanation, worked examples, guided practice, and a mini-assessment to track progress. We focus on building understanding rather than just memorization.

Our classes are primarily designed for Edexcel and AQA examination boards, but the core mathematical concepts and problem-solving techniques are applicable across all major exam boards including OCR and WJEC. We can provide specific exam-style questions for your particular board.

Progress is monitored through weekly mini-assessments during lessons. Parents receive monthly detailed feedback reports highlighting strengths, areas for improvement, and next steps. For Plus package students, additional feedback is provided on weekly homework tasks.

All sessions are recorded and made available in our online learning portal. Students can watch missed lessons and access the resources. For Plus package students, additional support is provided to help catch up on any missed content.

The Study Hub is our premium online resource library containing additional practice questions, video tutorials, worked solutions, and exam technique guides. It's designed to supplement the learning that happens during class sessions and provide extra support for independent study.
